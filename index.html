About

Hello! I’m Christopher, a front-end developer currently living in Valencia, Spain.

After graduating from Ulster University with a BSc in Computing Science, I rejoined the engineering team at Liberty IT in Belfast as a .Net developer and picked up where I had left off as an intern the year before. A year later, I decided to put my career on hold to work with an NGO in the Philippines and to travel. I currently live in Valencia, where I’m thrilled to call home, and feel like it's the perfect time to resume my career in either a front-end or full-stack role.

I enjoy creating things for the internet, from static web sites to full-stack applications, and am having a blast picking up where I left off a few years ago. As a self-motivated, hardworking person who enjoys learning and is able to get up to speed with new technologies quickly, I am confident that I can be an asset to any development team.

I am also open to working remotely, and would consider relocation within Spain for the right opportunity.
